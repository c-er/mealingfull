Our Mission

Around the world and even here at CMU, while some people do not have the means to even satisfy their hunger, others are wastefully extravagant. The excess food and leftovers of some can be a gold mine for others. Here at Mealingfull, our mission is to provide a means for information about this food to get to the people who really need it.

By using the technologies of machine learning and a live webcam, this site can let viewers know exactly what options are currently available.
